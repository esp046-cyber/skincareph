For Oily Skin

2

Cool compress, aloe vera gel generously, take ibuprofen for inflammation. Hydrate heavily. Avoid sun until healed completely.

5

Gently exfoliate, apply thick layer of moisturizer or Vaseline. Let sit 10 minutes. Tissue off excess before makeup.
